Sony V-4 Headphone

Description: The Sony MDR-V4 is a vintage pair of dynamic stereo headphones introduced around 1986, designed for both casual listening and professional monitoring. Weighing approximately 165 grams without the cord, they feature an adjustable headband and soft ear cushions for a comfortable fit, though the cushions may deteriorate over time. The headphones house 30mm dynamic drivers with a frequency response of 10 Hz to 25,000 Hz, an impedance of 45 Ohms, and a sensitivity of 103 dB/mW, ensuring clear highs and a pronounced midrange. They come with a 3-meter coiled cord for flexibility, a foldable design for easy storage, and an open-back structure with side vents that enhance the soundstage but provide limited noise isolation. While the MDR-V4 delivers reliable audio performance, its non-detachable cable and potential cushion wear are considerations for long-term users.
